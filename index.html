Add Faulty to your server!

Faulty is a Discord bot designed to give you information about your Fault account. Faulty allows you to check your ELO and other game states easily!



Using Faulty is easy! simply link your account on any server and it will be remembered on all servers!

Commands:

~add:

The ~add command is used to link your Fault account to your Discord profile. Simply navigate to your Fault profile and click on your username in one of your recent games. The page URL will now inlude your unique Fault ID.

Fault Url example: https://www.playfault.com/profile?player=48626

Syntax: ~add 48626

~stats:

The ~stats command tells Faulty to fetch your latest profile information, including Elo, ranking, wins, losses, and Win rate.
